About Me

I am a student at Carnegie Mellon University in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. I am working towards a BS in Statistics and Machine Learning, as well as a minor in Business Administration. Professionally, I am interested in going into either the Data Science or Medical fields.

In school, I have taken courses in Calculus 3, Probability Theory, Computer Science, and Business. I am also interested in Writing, which led me to take a course on technical writing this past semester. On campus, I serve as treasurer for the school's Indian spiritual organization, OM, and staffwriter for the news section of The Tartan, the school's student-run newspaper.

Academics aside, I enjoy spending time outdoors, traveling, reading, and watching movies or television. When I'm not in college, I'm usually back home in Ridgewood, New Jersey, with my parents, brother, and pet cat.
